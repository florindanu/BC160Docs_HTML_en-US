Visit Our Video Library

We know that some people prefer to watch content rather than read it. To that end, we're producing short, easily digestible videos that either stand alone, or supplement our user assistance.

If you are new to Business Central, you can launch the introduction videos from the welcome page that displays. If you're already working in Business Central, you can get to the library from the Business Manager and Accountant Role Centers by choosing the Product Videos tile.

You can also view our "how to" videos, plus a lot of other content, on the YouTube channel we created for Business Central. To visit the channel now, click here.

Tip

Videos in the library display in a frame that might seem a little small. To make the frame larger, choose the double-ended arrow icon in the upper-right corner. Afterward, videos will display in the larger frame until you make it small again.
